About Clock Town Studio

Professional music production. Over a decade of experience. $20 an hour.

Clock Town Studio (formerly The Hut Studio out of Shelton, CT) is a recording studio specializing in Pop and Hip-Hop music production. We are within 15 minutes of Waterbury, CT and 30 minutes of Danbury!

Our head engineer, Jake DeGennaro, has 15 years of experience working in the professional audio industry. He learned from some of the best while interning in various studios across the north-east, from New York City to Bristol. He has since worked in numerous professional studios, becoming the “go-to” guy for many artists when they needed someone with extensive pro-tools digital editing and advanced mixing skills. Clock Town will work with anyone from beginners looking to record a demo to seasoned professionals looking to record an album. Check Jake and his skills out in this video, made at The Hut.

Cassette to MP3/CD conversion, and VHS to DVD conversion also available.
